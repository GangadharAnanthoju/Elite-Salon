Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Elite Saloon ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — name, email address, and phone number when you make a booking or contact us.
  • Booking information — preferred services, barber preferences, and appointment history.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent on site, and browser type, collected via analytics tools.
  • Chat data — messages sent through our AI assistant are processed to generate responses and are not stored permanently.

How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm and manage your appointments.
  • To send appointment reminders and follow-up communications.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To respond to your enquiries.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Calendly — for online appointment booking. Subject to Calendly's Privacy Policy.
  • Microsoft Azure — for website hosting and AI assistant services.
  • Google Analytics — for website usage analytics (if enabled).

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@elitesaloon.com.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

Cancellation Policy

  • We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling.

Payments

Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how you use it.

Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function correctly. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use the site (e.g. pages visited, time on site). Used via Google Analytics.
  • Third-party cookies — set by Calendly when you use the booking widget.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. For more information on managing cookies, visit aboutcookies.org.
